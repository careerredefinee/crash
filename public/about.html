Our Story

Founded in 2023, Career Redefine was born from a simple yet powerful idea: that everyone deserves to love what they do. Our founder, Siddhu Mathapathi, experienced firsthand the frustrations of navigating the modern job market. He saw a need for a more intelligent, personalized, and supportive approach to career development.

He started as a small team of passionate innovators, and have grown into a global platform that has helped thousands of individuals transform their careers. We believe in the power of technology to create opportunities and the power of human connection to guide them.

Our Mentors
